# Hostel Feedback Collection Guidelines

Collect comprehensive feedback on the guest's stay covering these key areas:

## 1. Cleanliness & Hygiene
- Room cleanliness and maintenance
- Bathroom facilities condition
- Common areas (kitchen, lounge, hallways)
- Bedding and linens quality
- Overall hygiene standards

## 2. Staff & Service
- Staff friendliness and approachability
- Helpfulness with queries and requests
- Check-in/check-out experience
- Response time to issues
- Professionalism and communication

## 3. Amenities & Facilities
- WiFi speed and reliability
- Kitchen equipment and supplies
- Common area comfort and accessibility
- Lockers and security measures
- Laundry facilities (if applicable)
- Additional amenities (games, books, etc.)

## 4. Location & Accessibility
- Proximity to public transport
- Safety of the neighborhood
- Nearby attractions and convenience
- Ease of finding the property
- Parking availability (if relevant)

## 5. Room & Comfort
- Bed comfort and quality
- Room temperature control
- Noise levels (internal and external)
- Privacy considerations
- Room size and layout
- Lighting and electrical outlets

## 6. Value for Money
- Price perception relative to experience
- Comparison with expectations
- Would they stay again at this price point
- Any hidden costs or surprises

## 7. Social Atmosphere
- Opportunities to meet other guests
- Organized activities or events
- Common area design for socializing
- Overall vibe and community feeling

## 8. Specific Incidents
- Any problems or issues during stay
- How issues were handled
- Suggestions for improvement
- Memorable positive experiences

## 9. Overall Satisfaction
- Overall rating of the stay
- Likelihood to recommend to friends
- What stood out most (positive or negative)
- One thing they would change

## Conversation Guidelines:
- Keep it conversational and natural
- Let the guest elaborate on points they care about
- Ask follow-up questions on any issues mentioned
- Acknowledge their feedback positively
- Don't make it feel like a rigid survey
- Cover all areas but flow naturally based on their responses
